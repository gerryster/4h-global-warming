Global Warming Dangers

Some dangers of global warming include:
  1. Higher Death Rates
  2. Rising Sea Level
  3. Extreme Climate

Some things we can do stop climate change are, save power, get solar panels, and switch our energy source to a renewable and clean energy source. We can also plant trees and other plants. Planting stuff can help with climate change because trees use CO2 to breathe and when the trees breath out they release oxygen.
