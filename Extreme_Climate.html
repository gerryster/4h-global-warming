Extreme Climate

There will be extreme climate because when we pump out Co2 the Co2 will get trapped in the earth's atmosphere then when the heat from the sun goes on to the earth the heat bounces of the earth's crust then when the heat goes back up the Co2 will make the heat bounce back down to the earth then the heat will go back down to the earth bounce off of the earth and so on and so forth. This will heat up the earth.

Source: "The Greenhouse Effect." NASA Science Space Place. Accessed on 2021-07-22: https://spaceplace.nasa.gov/greenhouse/en/
